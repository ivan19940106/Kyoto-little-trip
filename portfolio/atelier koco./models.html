板材

使用５Ａ級木材開料，自由選擇的面、側、背板材搭配，創造出獨一無二的聲音

桶身

專攻ＯＭ和其變化形狀的桶身，ＳＪ、ＧＡ桶身的飽滿低頻和突出的高頻，非常適合演奏風格

力木架構

在傳統的X bracing架構下，輔以特殊角度的side bracing，將延音推展至極致，讓吉他在您的手裡歌唱

鑲嵌

除了極具說服力的聲音，我們的琴在視覺上也獨一無二，無論是名字、花鳥樹木還是圖騰，都可以做鑲貝裝飾

Carrera SJ

It is the most popular model of my guitars. SJ (Small Jumbo) is easy for you to hold under your arm and also has good balance of sound because its waist is thinned but still keeps body volume.

The design is based on padauk’s blazing red color. The thickness of the top is adjusted including the pick guard. Spanish style through-neck construction with a very thin heel at the joint, beveled cutaway and so on. I pursue its playability and sound. It even keeps on evolving.

Top Spruce
Side & Back Rosewood
Neck Mahogany
Spanish style through-neck construction with a very thin heel at the joint.
Fret Board Ebony
Bridge Jacaranda
Head Plate Spalted Maple
Tuners Gotoh 510Z Cosmo Black
Decorations Abalone
Other features Beta-titanium truss rod
It is lighter and springier than the titanium truss rod I used before. It specially works on the weight balance of the neck.
  • Please feel free to inquire about the kind of woods used for the top/side/back.
